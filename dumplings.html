Homemade Dumplings

This is a very simple recipe, detailing how to make dumplings at home, from scratch!

This recipe is very simple. As someone who absolutely loves dumplings, whether it be chicken and dumplings or just dumplings by themselves, this is a perfect quick and easy recipe for all you dumpling lovers out there!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • ½ cup milk

Steps

  1. Stir baking flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl (use amounts listed in the ingredients section)
  2. Cut in a tablespoon of margarine or butter until the mixture is crumbly
  3. Stir in the ½ cup milk and mix, a batter will form
  4. When the batter is thick enough to be picked up by a spoon, allow it to rest for 3-5 minutes
  5. Drop spoonfuls of batter into boiling water or stew and cover with a lid.
  6. let simmer for 15 minutes without lifting up the lid and serve
