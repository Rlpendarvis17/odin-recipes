Grilled Cheese

This is a quick and easy to make grilled cheese recipe!

For this recipe, we will show you how to make an easy and delicious grilled cheese at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat, and generously butter 1 slice of bread
  2. Place bread butter side down in the heated up skillet; add 1 slice of cheese
  3. Butter the second bread slice on one side, and place butter side up o top of the cheese
  4. Cook until lightly brown on one side; flip and continue until cheese is melted. Then repeat with the remaining slices of cheese, bread, and butter!
