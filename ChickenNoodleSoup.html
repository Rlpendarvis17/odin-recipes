Chicken Noodle Soup

Delicious Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe!

Another easy recipe, this time we're going to be looking at a staple of comfort food; an absolute classic!

This Chicken Noodle Soup recipe is sure to warm you up when it's cold putside and to leave you feeling full!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat
  2. Add onion and celery, cook until just tender (aboout 5 minutes)
  3. Add chicken and vegetable broth, and also add carrots, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt and pepper
  4. Stir these ingredients and bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, then serve!
